What does this test measure?

The goal of the test is to find the object that fits into the square marked with the question mark in the grid. The test thus measures your logical reasoning ability. Logical reasoning is of utmost important for certain jobs.

How to interpret your result

Your performance score has been calculated out of your correct and incorrect indications on the grid. Please consider that this is only a snapshot of your performance which can be influenced by various factors. Your performance in the test can be described as follows:

It is as easy for you as for other participants to handle the tasks in this test successfully.

What is your processing style like?

In addition to the performance score your processing style is measured. It indicates how many questions you completed in the default time (speed) and how many of those questions you answered correctly (accuracy). These two scores are compared with the scores of other participants as well. The optimal processing style is to complete as many questions as possible, as accurately as possible.

How can my skills in this area be improved?

Improving your skills of logical reasoning is not easy and requires commitment over a long period of time. Here are some tips to help you to improve your skills in this area:

  • Purchase books or magazines that contain puzzles with tasks such as the continuation of chains of numbers or filling in the missing pieces of a matrix. Solve these tasks on a regular basis.
  • Try to establish the rules in diagrams, data or chains of numbers when reading the News.
  • Play Sudoku on a regular basis.
